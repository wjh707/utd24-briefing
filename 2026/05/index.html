Greenwashing Under Competition

竞争环境下的绿色漂洗行为
(Multiple Authors)
▎English Abstract
Growing consumer awareness of corporate social responsibility (CSR) has motivated firms to invest in CSR initiatives to gain a competitive edge. However, a phenomenon known as greenwashing — where firms exaggerate their environmental commitments — has become a significant concern. This study examines how competition affects firms' greenwashing behaviors and their market consequences.
▎中文摘要
消费者CSR意识的提升推动了企业投资社会责任以获取竞争优势，但"绿色漂洗"——夸大环保承诺——已成为突出问题。本研究探讨竞争如何影响企业绿色漂洗行为及其市场后果，为监管和治理提供理论依据。

Investor Reliance on ESG Ratings and Stock Price Performance

投资者对ESG评级的依赖与股价表现
(Multiple Authors)
▎English Abstract
We exploit a quasi-natural experiment — the change in Sustainalytics' ESG rating methodology which reassesses risk and inverts the rating scale — to examine how investors rely on ESG ratings. The findings reveal significant stock price reactions to methodology-driven rating changes, highlighting the market impact of ESG information intermediaries.
▎中文摘要
利用Sustainalytics ESG评级方法论变更的准自然实验——重新评估风险并反转评级尺度——本研究揭示投资者如何依赖ESG评级。评级方法论驱动的变化引发了显著的股价反应，凸显了ESG信息中介的市场影响力。

Collaboration Post-Acquisition: The Role of Acquirers' Motives

▎English Abstract
