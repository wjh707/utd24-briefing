Carbon Pricing versus Green Finance

Multiple Authors
▎English Abstract
Green finance—including ESG investing and sustainable finance regulations—is widespread, but can it substitute for carbon pricing? This study compares the effectiveness of carbon pricing mechanisms versus green finance approaches in reducing emissions, finding that carbon pricing remains more efficient while green finance plays a complementary role in mobilizing capital toward sustainable investments.
▎中文摘要
绿色金融（ESG投资与可持续金融监管）虽广泛推行，但能否替代碳定价？研究比较碳定价机制与绿色金融方法在减排方面的有效性，发现碳定价仍更高效，而绿色金融在引导资本投向可持续投资方面发挥补充作用。

Nudging Green-but-Slow Shipping Choices in Online Retail

助推绿色慢速配送：在线零售中的环保配送选择
Multiple Authors
▎English Abstract
Faster e-commerce fulfillment often comes at a cost to the environment due to energy-intensive transportation and less consolidated shipments. This study tests behavioral nudges to encourage consumers to choose slower, greener shipping options, finding that framing environmental impact in concrete terms significantly increases green shipping adoption without reducing customer satisfaction.

The Effect of Online Cart Composition on Cart Abandonment

在线购物车构成对弃购行为的影响
Multiple Authors
▎English Abstract
Online shopping cart abandonment is widespread, causing major revenue losses. This research expands understanding by examining how the composition of items in a shopping cart—hedonic versus utilitarian products, and their mix—affects abandonment decisions. The findings reveal that cart composition significantly influences purchase completion through psychological mechanisms of choice justification and anticipated regret.
▎中文摘要

Multiple Authors
▎English Abstract
