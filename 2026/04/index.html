▎English Abstract
This study examines whether mandating ESG disclosure affects firms' competitiveness for foreign government procurement contracts. Findings show that ESG disclosure mandates improve firms' success in winning foreign government contracts, particularly for firms from countries with weaker domestic ESG norms, suggesting a regulatory spillover effect.

Mandatory Disclosure and Takeovers: Evidence from Private Banks

Public financial information plays a critical role in the takeover market by helping acquirers search for and value potential targets. Information Systems Research

Prompt Adaptation as a Dynamic Complement in Generative AI Systems

提示词适应：生成式AI系统中的动态互补
Eaman Jahani, Benjamin S. Manning, Joe Zhang et al.
▎English Abstract
Do users automatically benefit when generative AI models improve, or does realizing those gains require changing how they interact with the technology? Experiments with 3,750 participants and 37,000 prompts across two DALL-E versions reveal that benefiting from model improvements depends critically on task structure—in bounded tasks users gain automatically, while in open-ended tasks they must adapt their prompting strategies.
▎中文摘要
用户是否自动受益于生成式AI改进？3750名参与者提交近37000条提示的实验揭示，受益程度取决于任务结构——在边界明确的任务中用户自动获益，而开放式任务中须调整提示策略。

Tail-GAN: Learning to Simulate Tail Risk Scenarios

Tail-GAN：学习模拟尾部风险情景
Rama Cont, Mihai Cucuringu, Renyuan Xu et al.
▎English Abstract
The estimation of loss distributions for dynamic portfolios requires the simulation of realistic joint dynamics. We propose Tail-GAN, a novel data-driven approach using generative adversarial networks for simulating realistic, high-dimensional multivariate scenarios that capture tail dependencies critical for risk management in financial and operational contexts.
▎中文摘要
动态组合损失分布估计需模拟真实联合动态。提出Tail-GAN，一种利用生成对抗网络模拟捕捉尾部依赖的高维多元情景的数据驱动方法，对金融和运营风险管理至关重要。
