Do Mutual Funds Respond to Mechanical Changes in ESG Ratings?

共同基金是否对ESG评级的机械变化做出反应？
Seungju Choi, Fabrizio Ferri, Daniele Macciocchi
▎English Abstract
Using a quasi-experimental setting, we study whether mutual fund investors respond to a purely mechanical change in ESG ratings—independent of concurrent changes in firms' actual ESG activities. We find that when a firm experiences a mechanical increase in ESG ratings, the probability of being selected by an ESG fund increases, suggesting that fund managers rely on ratings even when changes do not reflect real ESG improvements.
▎中文摘要
利用准实验设计，研究共同基金投资者是否对ESG评级的纯机械变化（独立于企业实际ESG活动变化）做出反应。发现当企业经历ESG评级的机械提升时，被ESG基金选中的概率增加，表明基金经理即使面对不反映真实ESG改善的评级变化也会做出响应。

Can Lending Hierarchies Balance Bias? The Role of Personal Environmental Values in Credit to Green Firms

贷款层级能否平衡偏见？个人环保价值观在绿色企业信贷中的作用
Di Bu, Matti Keloharju, Yin Liao et al.
▎English Abstract
How do bankers treat green firms? Using unique loan application and banker preference data from a mid-sized bank, we find that customer managers give more favorable recommendations to green firms, especially when they hold green values themselves. However, a minority of environmentally skeptical loan officers, aware through internal training that customer managers favor green firms, adjust recommendations downward, partially offsetting the bias—an effect we call "lending hierarchies balancing bias."
▎中文摘要
利用独特贷款申请和银行家偏好数据，研究发现客户经理对绿色企业给予更有利的推荐，尤其当自身持有环保价值观时。但少数持怀疑态度的信贷员通过内部培训意识到客户经理偏好绿色企业后，会向下调整建议，部分抵消了偏见——一种"贷款层级平衡偏见"效应。

Inventor Commingling and Innovation in Technology Start-up Acquisitions

发明人融合与科技创业企业收购中的创新
Qingqing Chen, David H. Hsu, David Zvilichovsky
▎English Abstract
We explore a form of postacquisition integration by which inventors from the target and acquiring organizations share and integrate technological and organizational knowledge, performing joint R&D. Grounded in the knowledge-based view, we posit that commingling enhances the target firm's innovation performance by enabling the recombination and transfer of complementary knowledge between organizations.
▎中文摘要
探讨收购后整合的一种形式：来自被收购方和收购方的发明人共享并整合技术与组织知识，开展联合研发。基于知识基础观，认为发明人融合通过促进组织间互补知识的重组与转移，增强了被收购方的创新绩效。

CEO Compensation Changes Following Acquisitions

收购后的CEO薪酬变化
Leonce Bargeron, David J. Denis
▎English Abstract
We find that CEO compensation increases following acquisitions only in those deals in which acquirer stock is used as the method of payment. These compensation increases are driven by equity-based compensation and are concentrated in riskier acquirers, riskier acquisitions, and acquirers whose CEOs have low exposure to stock price.
▎中文摘要
发现CEO薪酬仅在以收购方股票为支付方式的收购后增加，主要由股权薪酬驱动，集中于风险较高的收购方、风险较大的收购交易以及CEO股价敞口较低的收购方。

Through an observational field study involving quantitative and qualitative components, we explore the role of gender diversity in shaping the effectiveness of entrepreneurial teams in attracting investment.
