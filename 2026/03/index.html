Effects of Mandatory Carbon Reporting on Greenwashing

强制碳报告对绿色漂洗的影响
Multiple Authors
▎English Abstract
We study the effects of mandatory environmental reporting on greenwashing, using a UK regulation requiring firms to disclose carbon emissions. Findings show that mandatory reporting reduces greenwashing by increasing transparency and accountability, but the effect is moderated by institutional factors and enforcement quality.
▎中文摘要
研究强制环境报告对绿色漂洗的影响，以英国要求企业披露碳排放的法规为背景。发现强制报告通过提升透明度和问责制减少绿色漂洗，但效果受制度因素和执法质量调节。

M&As Efficiency Gains: Evidence from Branch-Level Data

并购效率收益：来自分行层面数据的证据
Multiple Authors
▎English Abstract
We examine how banks reallocate employees following M&As and the resulting effects on productivity. Using matched employer-employee data at the branch level, findings reveal significant efficiency gains through employee reallocation, with post-merger productivity improvements concentrated in branches where workforce restructuring occurred.

MIS Quarterly

A Field Experiment in Local Personalization for Charitable Crowdfunding

慈善众筹中本地化个性化的田野实验
Multiple Authors
▎English Abstract
Charitable crowdfunding platforms use personalized outreach to engage donors. This field experiment tests how local personalization—framing donation appeals around geographic proximity—affects giving behavior. Results show that local framing increases donation rates but may crowd out support for distant causes, revealing a trade-off in personalization strategies.
▎中文摘要
慈善众筹平台利用个性化外展吸引捐赠者。本田野实验测试本地化个性化——围绕地理邻近性构建捐赠诉求——如何影响捐赠行为。结果表明本地化框架提高捐赠率但可能挤出对远距离事业的支持，揭示个性化策略的权衡。

When Market Incompleteness Is Preferable to Market Power: Insights from Power Markets

当市场不完全性优于市场力量：电力市场的启示
Multiple Authors
▎English Abstract
Since the liberalization of power markets, almost all investments require subsidies due to the incomplete nature of electricity markets. This study provides insights into market design, showing that under certain conditions, incomplete markets may yield better outcomes than markets dominated by powerful incumbents.
▎中文摘要
